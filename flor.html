Bio

Matt Ferr is a photographer and filmmaker based in Connecticut, USA. Matt’s unique lifestyle photography and branding expertise has built him a large following on instagram where he currently shares his latest creative work. His passion for travel and photography has led him across the world capturing unforgettable scenes. A major part of Matt's signature look comes from his ability to capture magical fleeting moments in captivating light.

Matt’s currently sponsored by Sony and takes part of Sony Alpha Imaging Collective, a coalition of the leading digital imaging creatives today. He’s done photo & film work with numerous brands, artists, models, concerts, tourism boards, nonprofits, large events, and more.
